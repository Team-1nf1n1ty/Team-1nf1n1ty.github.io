WHO ARE WE

We are Team 1nf1n1ty, The official cybersecurity club of Sastra Deemed to be University. We are college students who strive to work in research and innovation in the field of cybersecurity as well as in teaching, scientific dissemination, and knowledge transfer to society.

WHAT WE DO

Team 1nf1n1ty concentrates on playing Capture the flag (CTF). As per CTFtime.org, Team 1nf1n1ty currently ranks 29 in India. 1nf1n1ty is proficient in all the domains in CTF which makes the team rapidly grow. We post writeups that help the CTF community as well as beginners who are passionate about cybersecurity.
